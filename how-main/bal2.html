Lotto Chain – Decentralized Lottery & Betting

What is Lotto Chain?

Lotto Chain is a blockchain-based lottery and number betting platform that operates on smart contracts. With **verifiable randomness (VRF)** and decentralized lottery pools, all results are **transparent and tamper-proof**.

How to Play?

  1. 🎲 **Choose Your Bet Type** – Lotto Pools (HK, SG, etc.) or VRF Lottery.
  2. 📢 **Pick Your Numbers & Place a Bet** – Choose numbers for **6D, 5D, 4D, 3D, or 2D**.
  3. 📝 **Confirm & Submit** – Your bet is recorded on the blockchain.
  4. 🏆 **Check Results & Claim Winnings** – Payouts are automatically sent to winners.
